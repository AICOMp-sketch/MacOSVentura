macOS Ventura

Works magically. Does brilliantly.

macOS Ventura makes the things you do most on Mac better than ever. Powerful new features in your favorite apps. And new ways to work across your devices.

Powerful features. Designed for everything you do.

macOS Ventura brings a new level of power and productivity to your Mac with tools that transform how you work.

Stage Manager

Automatically organize your open windows so you can focus on your main task while still seeing your other windows in a single view.

Spotlight

Find and do things faster with rich results and Quick Look previews for files, images, and more throughout the system.

Mail

Schedule emails to be sent at the perfect time, undo send, and get reminders to follow up on messages.

Security

Advanced privacy protections and security features help keep your Mac safe from malware and other threats.

Continuity Camera

Use your iPhone as your Mac's webcam for stunning video quality and innovative features like Center Stage and Desk View.

Handoff

Start something on your iPhone or iPad and pick it up right where you left off on your Mac.

Designed for productivity and creativity.

A seamless experience that helps you focus on your work and express your ideas.

Work effortlessly across all your Apple devices.

With Continuity, you can start a task on one device and pick it up on another. Use your iPad as a second display with Sidecar, or sketch with Apple Pencil and see it instantly on your Mac.

Universal Control lets you work with a single mouse and keyboard across your Mac and iPad — no setup required.
